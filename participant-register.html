Information for Participants

Your details will be stored securely in a database maintained by the School of Sport, Exercise & Rehabilitation at Northumbria University. Your information will only be used to identify whether you may be eligible for research studies run by the School.

You will only be contacted if a study matches your profile. You may request removal of your details at any time by contacting sport.research@northumbria.ac.uk. Your data will not be shared with third parties outside of the University.

Data is held in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Northumbria University Data Protection Policy.
